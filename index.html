About Me

I am Dr. Nidhi Chaubey, the founder of Tooth & Co.

Dentistry is the perfect blend of science and artistry. My mission is to provide high-precision dental care using the latest advancements in the field—from smile designing to magnification-aided treatments.

I understand that dental anxiety is real for many. That is why I’ve designed my practice to be a sanctuary of gentle care, where modern technology meets a human touch. At Tooth & Co., we don't just treat teeth; we care for the people behind the smiles, ensuring that every procedure is conservative, durable, and tailored to your unique dental goals.
